FAQ

Most common questions, answered here

Is this really free?

Yes, both the versions of Sec-Voice Recorder are completely free to download and use.

It requires various permissions, how is it secure and safe?

It requires permissions just to save and get your recording files. Also requires internet permission to remind you if the update is available.

What features does lite version have?

Lite version has simply record and stop button functionality that saves your recording into SEC-VOICE folder.

What features does complete version have?

This version have record, play, rename, delete and share functionality within app. You will need not to go and find your recordings
